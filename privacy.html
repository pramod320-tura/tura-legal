Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Tura (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile app, website, and related services (collectively, the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, password, gender, date of birth, profession, and profile photo.
  • Profile Content: Status updates, interests, photos, and interactions you choose to share.
  • Usage Data: Device type, app activity, location (if enabled), and diagnostics for improving performance.
  • Payment Data: If you subscribe to Tura Premium, payment processing is handled securely by Apple or Google; we do not store payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, personalize, and improve the Service.
  • To connect you with other users through discovery, chats, and recommendations.
  • To communicate with you about updates, security, and new features.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and protect our users.
  • To comply with legal requirements.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Trusted cloud and analytics partners (e.g., Firebase, Google Cloud) under strict data protection terms.
  • Law enforcement, if legally required.

4. Data Retention

We retain user data as long as necessary to provide the Service or as required by law. You may request account deletion at any time via your in-app profile settings or by contacting support@tura.app.

5. Security

We use industry-standard encryption, secure authentication, and access control to protect user data. Despite these measures, no online service is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and correct your information in the app.
  • Request data deletion or export by emailing privacy@tura.app.
  • Opt out of marketing emails and notifications.

7. Children’s Privacy

Tura is not intended for users under 14. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a minor is using Tura, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant updates will be announced in-app or via email.

9. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@tura.app.
